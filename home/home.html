ABOUT CV. SMARTPLUS INDONESIA

CV.SMARTPLUS INDONESIA,was founded in 2015 with a focus on IT services as an application software developer to support the production process in the manufacture industry, along with our development we added services for the manufacturing process of electrical products using SMT machines by preparing 2 SMT lines for auto insert services for both prototype and mass production,as for the services we provide as follows:

  • IT Software Developer
  • Electronics Product Design prototype
  • Product Assembly and Mass Production

In addition to the above services, we also have a wire harness line equipped with auto wire cutting and crimping to complete the product electronics assembly process so that in the future we will be a one stop service for design and mass production assembly.
